Who We Are

Built by Athletes,
For Athletes

Slickland Ltd is a business created by former athletes, dedicated to empowering Rwanda's sporting community. We organise, manage, and elevate sports events that unite the nation.

From the flagship Rwanda Annual Sports Awards to the Ingenzi Sports Program, we bridge government, private sector, and non-profits to create a thriving sports ecosystem aligned with Vision 2050.

About RASA

Rwanda's Premier
Sports Movement

RASA is an annual sports awarding ceremony recognising outstanding talent, fostering excellence, and encouraging athletes and stakeholders to perform at their best.

This initiative unites all sectors — government, private sector, and non-profits — to create a thriving sports ecosystem in Rwanda. Organised by Slickland Ltd, created by former athletes.

The Process

How RASA Works

01
Nominations Open

Citizens and sports bodies nominate outstanding athletes across all categories via SMS and online platforms.

02
Public Voting

Over 2 million Rwandans vote via SMS and online — making RASA truly democratic.

03
Expert Panel Review

Sports experts and legends review nominations to confirm the final shortlist.

04
The RASA Gala

Winners announced at a world-class gala with live performances and global media coverage.
