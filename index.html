Who We Are

Built by Athletes,
For Athletes

Slickland Ltd is a business created by former athletes, dedicated to empowering Rwanda's sporting community. We organise, manage, and elevate sports events that unite the nation.

From the flagship Rwanda Annual Sports Awards to the Ingenzi Sports Program, we bridge government, private sector, and non-profits to create a thriving sports ecosystem aligned with Vision 2050.

Grassroots Program

The Ingenzi Sports Program

A nationwide initiative reaching all 30 districts of Rwanda, empowering youth through sports. Ingenzi means "champion" in Kinyarwanda — and that's exactly what we're creating.

Partnering with schools, local authorities, and sports federations to identify talent, provide coaching resources, and create pathways from grassroots to national competitions.

About RASA

Rwanda's Biggest Sports Night

The Rwanda Annual Sports Awards (RASA) is a flagship event by Slickland Ltd, celebrating Rwanda's sporting excellence every year at the Kigali Convention Centre.

RASA unites athletes, coaches, federations, government, and fans in one electrifying night of recognition, inspiration, and national pride.

The Process

How RASA Works

From nominations to the Gala night — here's how RASA celebrates Rwanda's champions

01
Nominations Open

Sports federations, clubs, coaches and the public submit nominations across all 9 award categories.

02
Public Vote

Shortlisted nominees go to a public vote. Every Rwandan gets a say in who takes home the award.

03
Expert Panel

A panel of sports experts reviews public votes and performance data to confirm the final winners.

04
RASA Gala Night

Winners are announced at an electrifying ceremony at the Kigali Convention Centre — July 10, 2026.

The Process

How RASA Works
